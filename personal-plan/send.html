So, how can I send a massage to the alumni?

If you have some idea to write, great! Keep moving! If you don’t, that’s fine. Let’s figure it out by learning from the examples.

There are two examples you can use as a reference.

  1. Ask broadly
  2. Ask specifically

-First example

Hi [First Name of Alumni], My name is [Your First Name], and I am currently studying [Your Degree] at BYU-Idaho. I am exploring career possibilities in the field and would love the opportunity to ask you a few question about your experience and insights. I would greatly appreciate if you have 15-20 minutes to talk with me at your convenience. Thank you for your time and consideration. Sincerely, [Your Name]

Key point!

  • Keep short and professional
  • Make it clear who you are, your purpose, and your request
  • Show respect and appreciation

-Second example

Hello [First Name of Alumni], My name is [Your Name], and I am a student at BYU-Idaho. I saw that you worked for [Company name] as [Position], and I would like to ask some questions about your career and experience. Will you be willing to speak with me for 15-20 minutes over this video chat? Thank you for your time considering. I look forward to hearing from you! Sincerely, [Your Name]

Key point!

  • Do not ask for a Job
  • Personalize the massage
  • Show respect and appreciation

Consider which of the example would most likely to fit you, then start writing!
